My Best Friend

by Rachatat BUnpat

My best friend is Tae. We first met when I moved into Suratpittaya School.

Tae is quite tall with short black hair and black eyes. He is very friendly and we got along very well. He is a patient guy and he always helps me when I need some help. Sometimes he needs recommendations about computers and I always help him with that.

He likes to play video games on his computer or mobile phone.

All in all, I think Tae is a very good friend.
